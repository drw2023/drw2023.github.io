Dewald Roode Workshop

The 15th Dewald Roode Workshop on Information Systems Security Research will continue the efforts to advance the discipline of information systems security through the creation, dissemination, and preservation of well-formed research.

The workshop is hosted by the University of Strathclyde and will take place on June 22-23, 2023 at the Rosslea Hall Hotel near Loch Lomond and Glasgow, Scotland.

Organizers

General Chair: Karen Renaud, University of Strathclyde, Glasgow, Scotland
Program Co-Chair: Jacques Ophoff, Abertay University, Dundee, Scotland
