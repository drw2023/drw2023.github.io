Register Here

You will be able to book extra nights at the hotel before and after DRW if they have space.

All meals will offer a vegetarian option

You will be able to pay an extra fee for a partner. This is to cover food and other overheads.

There will be two excursions (you will be able to check a box for these when registration opens):

  • A trip to Stirling Castle on the afternoon of the 21st for early arrivals. We will take a bus there (around an hour's drive) visit the castle, and return to the hotel in time for an evening meal.
  • A trip to a whisky distillery on Friday the 23rd in the afternoon. A bus will take us there so folks can feel free to taste the whisky and not worry about driving.
